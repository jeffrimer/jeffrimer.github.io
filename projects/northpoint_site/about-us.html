About NorthPoint Elementary School

NorthPoint Elementary School is a community of caring people that encourages positive self-esteem. The staff of NorthPoint Elementary School is dedicated to teaching and the student body ensuring that every individual has the right to achieve in a safe and positive environment.

NorthPoint Elementary has the largest enrollment of all the elementary schools in the district. It was built in 1972 and renovated in 2008 making it the top school of choice in the community.
